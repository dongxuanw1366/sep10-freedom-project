Construction



Context

Construction is something that started as far back as 400BC and Construction started with building shelters for humans to have a better living environment. Construction has been an important part of society for a long time because every building is a work of construction. This shows how construction is an important part of the world since without it we would have no shelter and no place to work. This is where my project comes in, the field of construction is a big topic and many workers have become injured or even died due to working in a construction field. My goal is to create something that can make construction an easier work and reduce the risk of injury and death, maybe even reduce it to zero if possible.

What would the future of construction look like?

Future of Construction

An easy to carry VR glasses that helps you stimulate a working field with 100% replication meaning you would actually feel like you are working in a real construction site (You won't feel over 50% pain). You even get to practice all kinds of construction tools so you can understand them better when you are actually going to use it. This tool would help you practice and get better at working, this product would reduce the overall accidents that might happen from working with inexperience.

The construction robot is essentially a robot that helps you build what you desired. This robot is equipped with highly advanced chip that would help them understand everything a human says and execute the task for them. It also has many built in tools(like hammer, drills, etc.) that would help speed up the process. The robot also knows how to use all kind of construction hardwares like bulldozers and trucks. This robot would be very helpful in reducing injuries happening from a construction site since humans wouldn't have to worry about doing work. Additionally, this robot can be connected with the VR headset in section 1. This means that the robot can be controlled by a VR, basically you will feel like controlling a character to work for you, the robot can copy online models of the building you want to build, as long as you have enough materials.

The nano toolpack is equipped with highly advanced nanotech. This toolpack essentially has every construction tool you ever need and you can carry it easily. It is shaped like a glove that will addjust to the size of your hand. The glove would have also help you have a strong grip allowing you to easily grab on stuff. This glove has all kind of tools minimized into nano size and stored in the glove and when you want to use it, you can open the ui interface to select a tool you want to use and it will appear on your hand. It can also spawn large tools like bulldozers, and boom lifts, etc. (You have to aim it at open space to put the tool down)
