Context

Construction has been an important part of society for a long time because for every building we see it is a work of construction. This shows how construction is an important part of the world since without it we would have no shelter and no place to work. This is where my project comes in, the field of construction is a big topic and many workers have become injured or even died due to working in a construction field. My goal is to create something that can make construction an easier work and reduce the risk of injury and death, maybe even reduce it to zero if possible.
